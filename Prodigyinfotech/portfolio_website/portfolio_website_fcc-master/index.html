Website Deployment

Website deployment involves the process of making a website accessible on the internet after development. It includes tasks such as configuring servers, uploading files, setting up databases, and ensuring the website runs smoothly. Deploying a website often involves using platforms like AWS, Heroku, or traditional web hosting services to make the site live and accessible to users worldwide.

DevOps

QA

QA, or Quality Assurance, involves ensuring that software meets specified requirements and standards before release. It includes testing functionalities, identifying bugs, and ensuring overall software quality through rigorous testing processes and methodologies.
